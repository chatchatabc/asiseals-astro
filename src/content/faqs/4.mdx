---
name: What are the most common causes of opened lapped seal faces?
order: 4
---

- The seal was set screwed to a hardened shaft.
- Solids in the product you are sealing are clogging the moveable components.
- The product changed state and interfered with the free movement of the seal.
  - The product crystallized.
  - It became viscous.
  - It solidified.
  - It built a film on the sliding components and the lapped faces.
  - The product vaporized across the lapped faces, blowing the faces open.
