Advantage Seal Type 16C Replaces:
AESSEAL® B05,
VULCAN® 14DIN,
Flowserve® 140
Designed for demanding applications, the Type 16C mechanical seal is a compact, unitized, single-spring elastomer bellows seal that offers maximum durability and performance.
The Type 16C is often found in water-based applications, such as wastewater treatment, potable water, HVAC, pool and spa and other general applications.
