Advantage Seal Type 37F Replaces:

AESSEAL® W02U,

ROTEN® UNITEN 7K,

VULCAN® 1688L

<br />

The TYPE 37F mechanical seal is BIDIRECTIONAL and
is characterized by a short axial length. It allows
both internal and external mounting,
It is ideal with high or pulsing counterpressure or with
viscous or sticky fluids
