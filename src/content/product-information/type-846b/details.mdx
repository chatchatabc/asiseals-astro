Advantage Seal Type 846B Replaces:

Eagle Burgmann® H7N,

AESSEAL® W07DMUB,

VULCAN® 1678

<br />

The type 846b machnical seal is widely used in pumps. It's not sensitive to low solids contents.

The transmission mode can be changed to provide maximum durability and performance.

It's very suitable for standard chemical pumps, vertical screw pumps, gear pumps and other optional equipment.

Commonly used in chemical industry applications, such as lubricating oil, medium with low solids content and other general applications.
