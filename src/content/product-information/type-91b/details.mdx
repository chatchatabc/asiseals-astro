Type 91B mechanical seal for pump is widely used, and it is a multi-spring PTFE wedge mechanical seal, which is used for general medium and low pressure work in processing, oil refining and petrochemical industries. Various materials are selected and combined to meet different media conditions.
Widely used in corrosive, low-temperature and high-temperature applications.
